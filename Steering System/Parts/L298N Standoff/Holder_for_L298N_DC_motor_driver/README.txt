# Summary

This little holder will help help you in your proyects, when testing or at the final stage in the
position at the area it is going to be located.
 The screws are 3 mm by 10 mm, the holes are a little big, but they work just fine. I load the 123D file
 just in case you need to change the size of the screw. Have fun.
